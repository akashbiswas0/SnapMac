# SnapMac

Snap your fingers to trigger actions on your Mac. Launch apps, run commands, toggle dark mode, and more. Lives quietly in your menu bar.

## Install

1. Go to the [Releases](../../releases) page
2. Download `SnapMac.zip`
3. Unzip it
4. Drag **SnapMac.app** into your `/Applications` folder
5. Right-click `SnapMac.app` and click **Open** (first launch only, macOS security warning)
6. Click **Open** again on the popup
7. Allow **Microphone** access when prompted in System Settings

## How to Use

Once running, a small icon appears in your menu bar. Click it to configure.

| What you do | What happens |
|---|---|
| Snap once | Triggers your Single Snap action |
| Snap twice quickly | Triggers your Double Snap action |

To set your actions:
- Click the menu bar icon
- Click **Set Action...** under Single Snap or Double Snap
- Choose from: App Launch, URL, Shell Command, Screenshot, Dark Mode toggle, Lock Screen, Mute

## Calibration (optional)

If snaps are not being detected reliably:

1. Click the menu bar icon and select **Calibrate**
2. Snap your fingers 5 times at normal strength
3. Sensitivity is automatically adjusted

## Troubleshooting

**Nothing happens when I snap**
- Go to System Settings > Privacy & Security > Microphone and enable SnapMac
- Try running Calibrate from the menu
- Switch to a different microphone via the Microphone menu

**It triggers too easily or not enough**
- Use the Sensitivity menu to adjust (Very High for quiet rooms, Low for noisy environments)

## Launch at Login

Click the menu bar icon and enable **Launch at Login** to start SnapMac automatically on login.

## Tips

- Calibrate first for best accuracy
- App names must match exactly (e.g. "Spotify" not "spotify")
- Double snap means two snaps within 0.5 seconds
